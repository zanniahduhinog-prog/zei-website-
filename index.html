ABOUT

Gender equality means equal access to opportunities and resources regardless of gender. It’s about ensuring fairness in education, work, leadership, and personal rights.

ISSUES

Despite progress, gender inequality still exists in many areas — from unequal pay and limited leadership roles, to social expectations and discrimination faced by women and the LGBTQ+ community worldwide.

TAKE ACTIONS

  • Promote equal education and leadership for all genders.
  • Speak up against discrimination and inequality.
  • Support organizations and campaigns that empower women and LGBTQ+ rights.
  • Celebrate diversity and create safe, inclusive spaces.
